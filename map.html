The Decision Tree

This guide is built around two decisions. The first shapes your budget. The second shapes your route. Everything converges on Cork for the weekend.

Landing: Dublin Airport, 4:20 AM Wed
Pick up rental car (automatic). Chase Sapphire covers CDW.
DECISION 1: Car Strategy
Round-trip from Dublin (~€180) or one-way drop in Cork (~€350)?
DECISION 2: Route to Cork (Wed–Fri)
Three ways to spend your first three days. All arrive in Cork by Saturday.
West Coast: Galway → Aran Islands → Cliffs → Cork
The epic route. Galway, Inis Mór, Burren, Cliffs of Moher, Doolin. Most driving, most variety.
Kerry & Dingle: Dublin → Dingle → Killarney → Cork
Slea Head Drive, Blasket Centre, Killarney National Park, Kenmare. The romantic route.
Slow South: Dublin → Kilkenny/Cashel → Cork
Extra time in Dublin, medieval Kilkenny, Rock of Cashel. Least driving, most time in Cork.
Cork & Kinsale Weekend (Sat–Sun)
All routes converge here. English Market, Kinsale, friends with a car, day trips.
Getting Home (Mon–Tue)
Car decision shapes this: drive M8 to Dublin or free day + train. Flight EI 0107 at 16:45 Tue.
