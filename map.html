Three Roads to Cork

Route A — Wild Atlantic Way (2 days)
Galway → Burren → Cliffs of Moher → Doolin → Dingle → Kerry → Cork
Most ambitious. ~10 hrs total driving. See the most, drive the most. Arrive Cork Saturday evening.
Clare Coast → Kerry & Dingle → Cork & Kinsale
Route B — Clare Shortcut (1.5 days)
Galway → Burren → Cliffs of Moher → Doolin → Limerick → Cork
See the Cliffs, skip Kerry. ~6 hrs total driving. Arrive Cork Friday evening.
Clare Coast → Cork & Kinsale
Route C — Direct Road (half day)
Galway → M18/M7/M8 → Cork
Most relaxed. ~3 hrs driving. Maximum time in Cork. Skip the coast entirely.
Cork & Kinsale
All routes converge
All three routes end in Cork for your weekend base. After Cork, the M8 corridor takes you back to Dublin for Tuesday's flight, with stops at Rock of Cashel, Cahir Castle, and Kilkenny along the way.
