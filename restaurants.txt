IRELAND TRIP - RESTAURANT RESEARCH
Late March 2026
===================================

All restaurants verified via web search March 2026.
Savannah = vegetarian-leaning pescatarian when out. Spencer = omnivorous.
Independent/local only. No chains.

KEY: $ = under 15, $$ = 15-30, $$$ = 30-50, $$$$ = 50+

DUBLIN - BREAKFAST/BRUNCH (for jet-lagged early arrivals)
================================================================

1. BROTHER HUBBARD NORTH
   153 Capel Street, Dublin 1
   Hours: Mon-Wed 9am-4pm, Thu-Fri 9am-4pm, Sat-Sun 9:30am-4pm
   (Dinner currently paused at this location)
   Price: $$ (brunch 11.50-16.50)
   Reservations: Walk-in for brunch
   Veg-friendly: Very. Middle Eastern-inspired menu. Seasonal Vegan Plate,
   sabiche, Turkish eggs (menemen), scrambled eggs with halloumi, homemade
   granola with tahini-honey drizzle. Extensive veg/vegan options alongside
   the Full Hubbard Irish breakfast.
   Why special: Opened 2012, 4 locations now. Middle Eastern influence with
   house-baked breads and cakes. One of Dublin's best-loved brunch spots.
   NOTE: Opens at 9am weekdays, 9:30am weekends. Not ultra-early.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated March 2026 on Yelp.

   Also: Brother Hubbard SOUTH (46 Harrington St) opens 8am Mon-Fri.

2. CORNUCOPIA
   19/20 Wicklow Street, Dublin 2 (off Grafton Street)
   Hours: Mon-Wed 8am-7pm, Thu-Sat 8am-8pm, Sun 9am-5pm
   Breakfast served 8:30am-12 noon
   Price: $-$$
   Reservations: Not needed
   Veg-friendly: 100% vegetarian/vegan restaurant since 1986. Three floors
   of a restored Georgian home. All food made fresh daily. Large gluten-free
   selection.
   Why special: Dublin's original vegetarian institution. 40 years running.
   Counter-service, cozy, unpretentious. Great for a quick wholesome
   breakfast without fuss.
   NOTE: Not the "excellent restaurant that happens to have veg options"
   brief -- it's fully vegetarian. But it's iconic and worth knowing about
   for Savannah especially.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated March 2026 on Yelp.

3. THE FUMBALLY
   Fumbally Lane, Dublin 8 (The Liberties)
   Hours: Tue-Fri 8am-5pm (kitchen closes 3pm), Sat 9am-5pm (kitchen 4pm),
   Sun 10am-3pm. CLOSED MONDAYS.
   Price: $$
   Reservations: Walk-in
   Veg-friendly: Strong veg and vegan options. Veganized Tuscan beans on
   house-made sourdough. Part cafe, part kitchen lab, part cultural space.
   Small market with fresh produce.
   Why special: One of Dublin's most original spots for conscious eating.
   Open-plan, neighborhood feel, great coffee. Weekday mornings are
   peaceful -- ideal for jet-lag recovery.
   NOTE: Opens at 8am Tue-Fri. Closed Mondays. Check day of arrival.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated March 2026 on Yelp.

4. ALMA
   Portobello, Dublin
   Price: $$
   Reservations: Recommended (book online)
   Veg-friendly: Vegan Hummus Toast with roasted garlic portobello
   mushrooms. Argentine flavors with Irish produce.
   Why special: Portobello neighborhood favorite. Popular, worth booking.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, listed in 2026 Dublin brunch guides.

DUBLIN - DINNER
================================================================

5. BASTIBLE
   111 South Circular Road, Portobello, Dublin 8
   Price: $$$$ (105 set menu)
   Reservations: Essential
   Veg-friendly: Dedicated vegetarian menu available with advance notice.
   Also accommodates pescatarian. Michelin-starred.
   Why special: Named after the traditional Irish cooking pot. Barry
   Fitzgerald and Claire-Marie Thomas's inventive seasonal cooking. Slightly
   out of center in Portobello but worth the trip. Ever-changing menu.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, listed in 2026 guides.

6. DELAHUNT
   39 Camden Street Lower, Dublin 2
   Hours: Tue-Sat dinner service
   Price: $$$ (2 courses 35-39, 3 courses 40-45)
   Reservations: Recommended via OpenTable
   Veg-friendly: "Incredible tasting menus for vegans and vegetarians."
   Seasonal, local produce. Traditional techniques -- home curing, smoking,
   slow-cooked braises.
   Why special: Housed in a beautifully restored Victorian pub from the
   19th century. Relaxed fine dining without pretension. Champions small
   local producers. Excellent value for the quality.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated February 2026 on Yelp/OpenTable.

7. LUCKY TORTOISE
   Temple Bar, Dublin
   Price: $$
   Reservations: Recommended
   Veg-friendly: Equal veg/vegan options as meat options. Even non-vegetarians
   choose the veggie all-in menu. Asian-inspired sharing plates.
   Why special: Fun, casual atmosphere. Great for a less formal evening.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, listed in current Dublin guides.

GALWAY - LUNCH & DINNER
================================================================

8. KAI CAFE + RESTAURANT  *** TOP PICK ***
   22 Sea Road, Galway (Westend)
   Hours: Lunch Mon-Sat 12-3pm (walk-in only). Dinner from 6:30pm (booking
   essential). Sunday brunch only 12-3pm. May close Sun & Mon -- verify.
   Price: $$-$$$ (Michelin Bib Gourmand)
   Reservations: Dinner only, essential. Book on website or call (091) 526003.
   Lunch is walk-in only. Online dinner bookings close at 3pm day-of.
   Veg-friendly: Many vegetarian options. Very accommodating for vegan/GF.
   Seasonal, locally sourced, often organic/wild ingredients. Daily changing
   menu.
   Why special: Arguably Galway's most impressive restaurant. Named from
   Maori word for food. No WiFi policy encourages connection. Stone floors,
   homely interior. Michelin Bib Gourmand.
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated February 2026 on Yelp. Michelin 2026 listing.

9. ARD BIA AT NIMMO'S  *** TOP PICK ***
   Spanish Arch, Galway
   Hours: Mon-Fri breakfast 10am-12pm, lunch 12-3pm. Weekends brunch all
   day. Dinner 6-9pm daily.
   Price: $$ (brunch ~22), $$$ (dinner)
   Reservations: Not needed for breakfast/lunch/brunch. Required for dinner.
   Veg-friendly: Eclectic Irish/Moroccan/North African menu. Wild mushroom
   tacos, maple roasted parsnips with apple butter and hazelnuts, polenta
   cake. Strong vegetarian selection.
   Why special: Stunning location overlooking Spanish Arch and River Corrib.
   Upstairs intimate dining room. Downstairs casual cafe. Seasonal menu
   from their own Burren garden. Est. 2000. Charges 2 per table for water
   (goes to tree planting).
   VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated March 2026 on Yelp.

10. DELA  *** TOP PICK ***
    51 Lower Dominick Street, Galway (Westend)
    Hours: Mon-Tue 11:30am-3pm. Wed-Thu 11:30am-3pm & 6-10pm.
    Fri-Sat 10am-3pm & 6-10pm. Sun 10am-4pm.
    Price: $$
    Reservations: Brunch walk-in only. Dinner (Thu-Sat) book online.
    Veg-friendly: Herb green falafel wrap, seasonal veg plates. Great
    vegetarian breakfast. Vegan/natural/organic wines.
    Why special: "Seed to feed" philosophy. They grow their own food and
    roast their own coffee at their farm in Moycullen. Scandinavian-inspired
    modern Irish. Famed brunch.
    Contact: Letseat@dela.ie (phones hard to reach)
    VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, updated March 2026 on Yelp.

11. THE PEAR TREE CAFE & WINE BAR (Salthill)
    Grattan Road, Salthill (GCI Building)
    Hours: Daily 8am-4pm. Thu-Sat also 4-10pm (wine bar evenings).
    Price: $$
    Reservations: Available via OpenTable
    Veg-friendly: Vegan, vegetarian, GF always available. Nearly 100% organic.
    Chef Laura Rosso. Specialty coffee, sourdoughs, generous plates.
    Why special: Overlooking the sea in Salthill. Cafe of the Year 2023
    (Connacht). Perfect if staying near Salthill. Wine bar evenings with
    natural wines and dinner plates.
    VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, listed on OpenTable and multiple 2026 guides.

ARAN ISLANDS (INIS MOR) - MARCH WARNING
================================================================

March is deep off-season. Many places have reduced hours or are closed.
CALL AHEAD for all of these.

12. TI JOE WATTY'S BAR & SEAFOOD RESTAURANT
    Kilronan Village, Inis Mor
    Hours (summer): Daily 12:30-9pm. Winter: food service may be intermittent.
    Bar open Mon-Fri from 2pm even when kitchen closed.
    Price: $$
    Reservations: Walk-in only (no reservations taken)
    Veg-friendly: "Many vegetarian options" per Yelp. Menu includes variety
    of vegetarian dishes alongside seafood.
    Why special: Heart of island life. Live Irish music. Local seafood from
    local fishermen. In winter, it's locals at the bar with pints by the
    fire -- authentic island pub experience.
    CONTACT BEFORE GOING: +353 86 049 4509 or joewattysbar@gmail.com
    STATUS: May have limited food service in March. Verify.

13. ARAN ISLANDS HOTEL (Madigans)
    Kilronan, Inis Mor
    Price: $$-$$$
    Veg-friendly: Menu highlights local seafood and "organically grown
    vegetables." Seasonal menu.
    Why special: Most reliable option for a proper sit-down meal in winter.
    Hotels tend to stay open when standalone restaurants close.
    STATUS: Seasonal business -- menu subject to change. Call ahead.
    CONTACT: Check aranislandshotel.com for March availability.

14. PIER HOUSE RESTAURANT
    Inis Mor
    Price: $$
    Why special: Good food, waterfront location.
    STATUS: Unverified for March. Call ahead.

RECOMMENDATION: If visiting Inis Mor in late March, eat a big breakfast

drinks at minimum. Don't count on having multiple dining options.

CORK CITY
================================================================

30. CAFE PARADISO  *** TOP PICK ***
    16 Lancaster Quay, Cork City
    Hours: Mon-Sat dinner from 5:30pm. Closed Sunday.
    (May also do lunch Tue-Sat 12-3 -- verify current schedule)
    Price: $$$$ (68 for 6-course set menu)
    Reservations: Essential. Book between 11am-4pm. Call for 5+ guests.
    No small children/infants.
    Veg-friendly: 100% VEGETARIAN restaurant. Ireland's most famous
    vegetarian fine dining. Vegan version always available (advance notice).
    GF adaptable. Seasonal, local vegetables front and center.
    Why special: A Cork institution and pilgrimage-worthy for vegetarians.
    Denis Cotter's legendary restaurant. Rich, complex flavors. Not a
    compromise -- this is destination dining.
    NOTE: While it IS a vegetarian-only restaurant, it's too iconic to
    leave off this list. Spencer can enjoy it too -- the cooking transcends
    dietary labels.
    VERIFIED OPEN: Yes, multiple 2026 listings confirmed.

31. MARKET LANE

    meat, salads. Seasonal/artisan ingredients from English Market.
